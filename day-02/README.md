## How It Works

1. Asks for the total bill amount
2. Asks for the tip percentage (10, 12, or 15)
3. Asks how many people are splitting the bill
4. Calculates the total bill including tip
5. Divides the total by number of people
6. Rounds to 2 decimal places and displays the result
